About

Hi! My name is Veronica.

NEWBORN MATERNITY FAMILY PHOTOGRAPHER

I am a Brisbane based newborn and family photographer specialising in newborn photography. I have my studio in Springfield Lakes, but will travel to your home for lifestyle and documentary sessions or your chosen outdoor location.

Photography sessions with me are usually relaxed and unrushed. You will hopefully feel like you are catching up with an old friend. You don’t need to worry about knowing what to do, I will give you as much or as little direction as required. I will work hard to make sure you get photos beautiful images you can pass down to your children and their children.

As a family photographer I am with you through the years. From newborn, maternity, baby and family photos spanning generations. I would be honoured to be the one who gets to capture these important stages in your life. My aim as a photographer is to keep the memory of childhood and family alive through photography.

When was the last time you updated your family photos or had your photo taken? Get in touch if you would like more information, or to have a chat about updating your family portraits.

I look forward to speaking with you and planning your photography experience!

Lots of love,

Veronica
